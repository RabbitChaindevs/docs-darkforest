# Create Your First Civilization

## Prerequisites

- MetaMask installed and connected to BSC mainnet
- **0.01 ~ 0.05 BNB** in your wallet (entry fee, which grows linearly over time from the minimum to the maximum)
- At least **1 DFT** (for subsequent upgrades)

## Creation Steps

### Step 1: Access the Game

Open **[darkforest.uk](https://darkforest.uk)** and click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top-right corner.

### Step 2: Name Your Civilization

The civilization name can be **1–32 bytes**, supporting letters, numbers, and symbols.

### Step 3: Enter an Invite Code (Optional)

If you have an inviter's wallet address, you can enter it here. Both you and the inviter will receive **150 Energy** as a reward.

### Step 4: Confirm Creation

Click **"Create Civilization"** — MetaMask will pop up a transaction confirmation. The transaction includes:

- **Entry fee**: 0.01 BNB (minimum, increases linearly to 0.05 BNB over 365 days)
- **Gas fee**: A small amount of BNB

Confirm and wait for the transaction to finalize on-chain (about 3–5 seconds).

## Initial State

After successful creation, your civilization starts with:

| Attribute | Initial Value |
|-----------|---------------|
| Energy | **2,000** |
| Health | **3,000** |
| Scan Range | **1,000** |
| All System Levels | **Lv 1** (Collector, Weapon, Shield, Radar, Engine) |
| Shield HP | **3,615** (3600 + 15 × 1²) |
| Position | Deterministic coordinates (derived from your address and a nonce) |

## Coordinate Assignment

Your civilization is assigned a **deterministic coordinate** `(x, y, z)`, computed via a hash of your wallet address, creation time, and the total number of civilizations. Each civilization's coordinates are unique.

## New Player Protection

> 🛡️ Newly created civilizations have a **24-hour** protection period. During this time, other players cannot attack you.
