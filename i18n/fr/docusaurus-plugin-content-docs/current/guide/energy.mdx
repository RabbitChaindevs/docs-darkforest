# Energy System

Energy is the most fundamental resource in Dark Forest. Almost every operation — upgrades, attacks, movement, and repairs — requires energy.

---

## Earning Energy

### 1. Energy Collector (Passive Output)

```
Collection Rate = 3 + 10 × √(collector level - 1)   energy/s
```

For each player you invited, the rate increases further.

**Collector Durability**: The collector loses 1 durability per second.

| Collector Level | Output per Second | Max Collector Durability |
|----------------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Lv 1 | 3 | 1 day |
| Lv 10 | ~33 | 1 day + 18 hours |
| Lv 50 | ~73 | 1 day + 4 days |
| Lv 100 | ~103 | 1 day + 8.3 days |

> ⚠️ When collector durability runs out, production stops. You need to repair it for collection to resume.

### 2. Combat Plunder

After winning a battle against another civilization, you plunder a portion of their energy:

```
Plunder Ratio = 5% + weapon level × 0.5%
```

Plundered energy goes into a **pending pool** (pendingCombatEnergy), which you must manually claim.

### 3. Invite Rewards

For each new player you successfully invite (they enter your address when creating their civilization):

- **You** receive 150 energy
- **The invitee** receives 150 energy

### 4. Energy Market

See [Energy Market](/docs/economy/energy-market).

## Spending Energy

| Use Case | Cost |
|----------|------|
| **Daily Upkeep** | `2,000 × total level` / day |
| **System Upgrades** | Anchor Formula (see Civilization guide) |
| **Attack** | `50,000 + 50,000 × weapon level` |
| **Space Jump** | `200,000 + 200,000 × √(jump count)`, cap 16.5M |
| **Repair Collector** | `1 energy / second` |
| **Repair Weapon** | `3 energy / point` |
| **Repair Shield** | `4 energy / point` |
| **Repair Engine** | `5 energy / point` |
| **Shield Regen** | `regen amount × 1` (auto regen) |
| **Shield Repair** | `repair amount × 4` (manual repair) |
| **Alliance Shield Assist** | `repair amount × 1` (paid by ally) |
| **Totem Donation** | Custom amount |
| **Rebuild** | Base 500,000, increases with rebirth count |

## Energy Management

### Daily Upkeep

Upkeep is deducted proportionally over time whenever you perform any action:

```
Current Upkeep = 2,000 × total level × (time since last action / 86400)
```

### Reserved Energy

Energy placed in sell orders on the energy market is locked (`energyReserved`). Upkeep stops being deducted once `energy - reserved` reaches zero.

### Tips

1. **Keep energy > 1 month's upkeep** — prevents frequent depletion from routine actions
2. **Prioritize collector upgrades** — a high-level collector is key to passive income
3. **Calculate costs before attacking** — make sure the plunder is worth the investment
4. **Repair collector durability regularly** — avoid collection interruptions
