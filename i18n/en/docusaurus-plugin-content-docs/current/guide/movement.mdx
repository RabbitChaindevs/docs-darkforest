# Space Jump & Cruise

## Cruise

Cruise is the standard method of movement. The engine propels your civilization toward a target coordinate.

### Conditions
- Engine durability > 0
- Target coordinates are within your radar range

### Operation

```
startMove(x, y, z)
```

Enter the target coordinates `(x, y, z)` to begin cruising. You can cancel at any time before arrival (`cancelMove`).

### Speed

```
Move Speed = 10 + 5 × engine level
```

The higher your engine level, the faster you move.

---

## Space Jump

A space jump instantly teleports you to a new deterministic coordinate. It is much faster than cruising but comes with serious side effects.

### Cost

Each jump's cost increases with **historical jump count**:

| Resource | Formula | Cap |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Energy** | `200,000 + 200,000 × √(historical jumps)` | 16,500,000 |
| **DFT** | `10 + 10 × √(historical jumps)` | 1,000 DFT |
| **Engine Durability** | 1 point | |

### Cooldown

After each jump, you must wait **1 hour** before jumping again.

### Side Effects

> ⚠️ Space jump inflicts heavy damage on your vessel:
>
> **Shield HP resets to 10% of maximum**
>
> Your defense will be extremely fragile after jumping. Make sure you jump in a safe area or prepare defensively.

### Coordinate Assignment

The new coordinates after a jump are assigned by a deterministic algorithm (similar to civilization creation). You cannot manually choose your destination.

---

## Tracking Jump

A tracking jump lets you jump directly to a target civilization's location.

### Conditions

- Your **radar level ≥ 20**
- The target is **within your radar scan range**

### Cost

Same as a regular space jump (energy + DFT + engine durability), and shares the same cooldown.

---

## Comparison

| Method | Cost | Speed | Use Case |
|--------|------|-------|----------|
| Cruise | Time only | Slow | Short-distance movement, exploration |
| Space Jump | Energy + DFT + Shield HP | Instant | Long-distance travel, evading tracking jumps |
| Tracking Jump | Energy + DFT + Shield HP | Instant | Chasing targets, assisting allies |

---

## Post-Alliance Non-Aggression Protocol

After leaving an alliance, you and the former alliance **cannot attack each other for 24 hours** (Leave Cooldown), preventing tactical abuse of the alliance system.
