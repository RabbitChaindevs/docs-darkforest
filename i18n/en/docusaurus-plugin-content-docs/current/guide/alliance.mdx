# Alliance System

## Creating an Alliance

### Requirements
- You must not belong to any current alliance
- Choose a name (1–32 bytes)

### Steps
1. Open the alliance panel
2. Click **"Create Alliance"**
3. Enter the alliance name
4. Confirm the transaction

The alliance ID is automatically generated by the contract (based on a hash of the timestamp, creator address, and name).

---

## Joining an Alliance

### Applying to Join
1. Browse the alliance list
2. Select an alliance
3. Click **"Join"**
4. Confirm the transaction

### Member Limit
Each alliance can hold a maximum of **100 members**.

---

## Leaving an Alliance

### Exit Penalty

Leaving an alliance requires paying a DFT fine:

```
Fine = 100 DFT + 10 DFT × current member count
```

The fine is distributed to remaining members as **pending refunds**, with any remainder going to the alliance leader.

### Exit Cooldown

After leaving, you and the former alliance **cannot attack each other for 24 hours** (Leave Cooldown). This prevents players from repeatedly joining and leaving alliances to evade combat.

---

## Kicking Members

The alliance leader can kick members:

- Kicked members also enter a **24-hour exit cooldown**
- Kicked members must pay the same exit fine

---

## Alliance Management

| Action | Permission | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Transfer Leadership | Leader | Transfer to another member |
| Disband Alliance | Leader | Removes all members, deletes the alliance |
| Kick Member | Leader | Gas cost increases with member count |
| Claim Refunds | Member | Claim DFT fines left by departed members |

---

## Alliance Defense Bonus

Alliances provide additional defensive power:

```
Alliance Defense Bonus = (online allies - 1) × 8 × (1 + totem level × 0.5%)
```

This means:
- The more allies you have, the higher everyone's defense
- Higher totem levels amplify the defense bonus from allies

---

## Disbanding an Alliance

The leader can disband the alliance:
- All members are removed
- Members can still claim pending refunds
- Alliance data is deleted from the chain
