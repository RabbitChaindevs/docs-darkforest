# Get DFT Tokens

**DFT (Dark Forest Token)** is the core ERC-20 token of Dark Forest, with a total supply cap of **420.69 trillion**.

**DFT Uses:**
- 🚀 Space jumps (10–1,000 DFT per jump)
- 🔧 System upgrades
- 🔄 Rebuilding a civilization
- 🏛️ Alliance exit fines
- ⚡ Energy market trading

---

## Method 1: Buy on a DEX

DFT is listed on PancakeSwap.

### Steps

1. Open [PancakeSwap](https://pancakeswap.finance/swap)
2. Connect your wallet
3. In the "To" field, enter the DFT contract address:

```
0x1266e922fe34459efda34e7ee5caf327fbf138d7
```

4. Enter the amount and click **"Swap"**

> 💡 **Note**: LP trades on PancakeSwap incur a **2.5% transaction tax** (1% development fee + 1.5% marketing fee).

---

## Method 2: Daily Mint (Free)

Claim a free amount of DFT every day:

1. Open [darkforest.uk](https://darkforest.uk)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Click **"Daily Claim"**
4. If `distribute()` hasn't been called yet for the day, you can trigger it yourself
5. Confirm the transaction (gas fee only)

> ⚠️ **Claim it the same day or lose it.** The DailyMinter does not accumulate unclaimed DFT — any leftovers are burned when the next day's distribution runs.

## Method 3: Community Events

Follow the official Discord / Telegram for occasional airdrops and community reward events.

## View Your DFT Balance

To add DFT to MetaMask:

1. Open MetaMask → **"Import Tokens"**
2. Contract address: `0x1266e922fe34459efda34e7ee5caf327fbf138d7`
3. The symbol will auto-display as **DFT**, with **18** decimals
4. Click **"Add"**
