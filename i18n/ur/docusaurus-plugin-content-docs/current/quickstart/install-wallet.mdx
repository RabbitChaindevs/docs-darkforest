# Install a Wallet

Before you can play Dark Forest, you need a Web3 wallet to manage your digital assets and sign on-chain transactions.

## What Is a Web3 Wallet?

A Web3 wallet is your key to decentralized applications (dApps). It lets you:

- **Store and manage** digital assets like DFT and BNB
- **Sign transactions** — every in-game action (building, moving, fighting) requires your wallet's signature
- **Authenticate your identity** — your wallet address is your identity in Dark Forest

## Recommended Wallets

### MetaMask (Recommended)

MetaMask is the most popular Web3 wallet, available as a browser extension and mobile app.

#### Browser Installation

1. Visit the [MetaMask website](https://metamask.io)
2. Click "Download" to get the browser extension
3. Choose your browser (Chrome / Firefox / Edge / Brave)
4. Click "Add to Browser"
5. Follow the wizard to create a new wallet

:::tip
After installing, pin the MetaMask extension to your browser toolbar for quick access.
:::

#### Creating a Wallet

1. Click the MetaMask icon and select "Create a Wallet"
2. Set a **strong password** (at least 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers)
3. **Back up your seed phrase securely** — this is the only way to recover your wallet!

:::danger Seed Phrase Security
- **Never** share your seed phrase with anyone
- **Do not** take screenshots or store it in the cloud
- Use physical media (paper and pen) for offline backup
- Anyone asking for your seed phrase is a scammer
:::

4. Confirm your seed phrase to complete wallet creation

### Trust Wallet

Trust Wallet is Binance's official mobile wallet.

1. Search for "Trust Wallet" on the App Store or Google Play and install it
2. Open the app and select "Create a New Wallet"
3. Securely back up your seed phrase
4. Enable the DApp browser within the wallet

## Next Steps

Once your wallet is installed, proceed to [Connect to BSC](/docs/quickstart/connect-bsc) and get ready to enter Dark Forest.
