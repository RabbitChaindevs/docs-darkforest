# Civilization Development & Upgrades

Your civilization is made up of **six systems**, each of which can be independently upgraded. System levels determine your collection efficiency, combat power, and survivability.

---

## The Six Systems

| System ID | Name | Function | Core Formula |
|-----------|------|----------|--------------|
| 0 | **Energy Collector** | Passive energy per second | `3 + 10 × √(level - 1)` energy/s |
| 1 | **Weapon System** | Attack power | `900 + 10 × level²` |
| 2 | **Shield System** | Defense & shield HP | HP: `3600 + 15 × level²`, Defense: `540 + 6 × level²` |
| 3 | **Radar System** | Scan range | `1000 + 150 × level + 5 × level²` |
| 4 | **Engine System** | Movement speed | `10 + 5 × (level-1)` → Lv1=10, Lv10=55 |

---

## Upgrade Costs

Upgrade costs are calculated using the **Anchor Formula**, based on the system type and current level:

### Level Brackets

| Level Range | Anchor Days (cost equivalent) |
|-------------|-------------------------------|
| 1 – 100 | 1 → 30 days of energy/DFT output |
| 100 – 1000 | 30 → 60 days of output |
| 1000+ | 60 days of output (capped) |

### System Upgrade Multipliers

| System | Energy/DFT Multiplier |
|--------|----------------------|
| Collector (0) | 1× |
| Weapon (1) | 4× |
| Shield (2) | 4× |
| Radar (3) | 8× |
| Engine (4) | 16× |

### Upgrade Cost Formula

```
Upgrade Cost = Anchor Days × Daily Output × System Multiplier
```

"Daily Output" scales with your civilization's level — the higher your level, the more you produce per day, creating a **positive feedback loop**.

---

## Daily Upkeep

Your civilization consumes energy every day as maintenance:

```
Daily Upkeep = 2,000 × Total Level
```

Upkeep is deducted from your energy proportionally over time and is settled automatically whenever you perform any action (via `_deductUpkeep`).

> ⚠️ If you don't have enough energy to cover upkeep, your systems will still function, but your energy balance will go negative. You'll need to replenish it promptly.

---

## Durability System

Each system has durability that decreases over time or with use:

| System | Base Durability | Per-Level Increase |
|--------|----------------|--------------------|
| Collector | 86,400 seconds (1 day) | +7,200 seconds (2 hours) |
| Weapon | 60 | +15 |
| Shield | 40 | +10 |
| Engine | 30 | +6 |

### Durability Consumption

- **Collector**: decays over time, losing 1 durability per second
- **Weapon**: loses 1 durability per attack (at 0 durability, attack power is halved)
- **Shield**: loses 1 durability each time it's hit
- **Engine**: loses 1 durability per jump

### Repair Costs

| System | Repair Cost |
|--------|-------------|
| Collector | 1 energy/second |
| Weapon | 3 energy/point |
| Shield | 4 energy/point |
| Engine | 5 energy/point |

---

## Upgrade Strategy Tips

1. **Prioritize the Collector** — energy is the foundation of everything. A high-level collector lets you accumulate resources faster
2. **Balance Weapon and Shield** — Weapon level affects attack power and plunder ratio; Shield level affects HP and defense
3. **Radar determines your attack range** — you can only attack targets within your radar's reach
4. **Engine affects movement speed** — exploration and pursuit both depend on engine upgrades
