DFT Token Overview

DFT (Dark Forest Token) is the core token of the Dark Forest ecosystem, implemented as a standard ERC-20 token.

Basic Information​

PropertyValue
Contract Address0x1266e922fe34459efda34e7ee5caf327fbf138d7
Token NameDarkForest
SymbolDFT
Decimals18
Total Supply Cap4,206,900,000,000 DFT (~4.2 trillion)
Distribution Period~10 years (3,650 days)

DFT Uses​

Use CaseDescription
🚀 Space Jump10 + 10 × √(jump count) DFT, cap 1,000
🔧 System UpgradesAnchor formula-based upgrade costs
🔄 Rebuild Civilization100 + 10 × rebirth count DFT, cap 2,000
🏛️ Alliance Exit Fine100 + 10 × member count DFT
⚡ Energy MarketBuy energy via order book using DFT

Tokenomics​

Supply​

  • Hard cap: 4,206,900,000,000 DFT (~4.2 trillion)
  • Daily mint: 4,206,900,000,000 / 3,650 ≈ 1,152,575,342 DFT (constant daily emission)
  • Daily distribution: via DailyMinter to active players

Transaction Tax​

When DFT is traded on a DEX (PancakeSwap):

TaxRatePurpose
Development Fee1%Team
Marketing Fee1.5%Marketing
Total Transaction Tax2.5%

Tax only applies when trading in registered liquidity pools (LP). Regular transfers are untaxed.

Viewing DFT in Your Wallet​

To add DFT to MetaMask:

  1. Open MetaMask → "Import Tokens"
  2. Contract address: 0x1266e922fe34459efda34e7ee5caf327fbf138d7
  3. The symbol will auto-display as DFT
  4. Click "Add"
